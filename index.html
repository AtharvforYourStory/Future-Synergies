Collaborative Framework

This partnership is part of the broader "The Bharat Project" initiative by YourStory. Below are the defined roles to ensure a successful and mutually beneficial collaboration.

Role of the Incubator

  • Support outreach and promotion of initiatives within its founder ecosystem.
  • Nominate promising startups for national showcases and events.
  • Facilitate access for story shoots, interviews, and documentation.
  • Enable The Bharat Project to plug into startup cohorts via AI Co-Pilot & masterclasses.
  • Collaborate on co-hosting regional founder-led events (where feasible).
  • Share non-confidential aggregated data to enrich our knowledge base.

Role of YourStory

  • Act as the knowledge and execution partner for The Bharat Project.
  • Create and deploy startup-focused digital content, tools, and formats.
  • Organize the Build for Bharat Fellowship and mentorship programs.
  • Curate and document 2,000+ high-potential startups from across the country.
